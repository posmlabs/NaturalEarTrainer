Privacy Policy for the NaturalEar App

The NaturalEar - Ear Trainer app does not collect or process any personal data of the user.

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous user data (e.g., ad identifiers) for the purpose of personalizing and analyzing ads. Details about how AdMob processes data can be found in the Google Privacy Policy.

The app also uses Firebase Crashlytics, which collects anonymous information about errors and crashes to improve the app's performance. This data does not contain any information that can identify the user.

All data saved within the app (e.g., settings or progress) is stored locally on the user's device.

By using the app, you accept the above terms.
